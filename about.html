About College Events Website

This College Events Website is your one-stop for finding out about all the exciting events happening on campus! This website is designed to help you easily find and RSVP to events, and to help event organizers easily promote their events to the campus community.

The team is currently made up of just one student (!) who is passionate about creating a vibrant and engaged campus community. I believe that events are a great way to bring people together, and I'm committed to helping make our college experience the best it can be!
